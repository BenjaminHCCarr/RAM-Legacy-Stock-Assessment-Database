Ecology

Assessed species span a range of ecological traits. Some life-history information (e.g. growth, maturity, fecundity) is available for 288 of the collated assessments. In some cases, this information is derived from biological studies, while in other cases life-history parameters represent model assumptions (e.g., natural mortality = 0.2) or model estimates. The trophic level of assessed species ranged from 2 to 4.5 with a mean of 3.7, with no apparent relationship between trophic level and stock status (Figure 4).
