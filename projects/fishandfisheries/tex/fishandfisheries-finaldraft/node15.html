Timespan

The median lengths of catch/landings, SSB, and recruitment timeseries were 38, 34, and 33 years, respectively (Figure 5). The time period covered by 90% of assessments is: catch/landings (1967-2007), SSB (1972-2007), recruitment (1971-2006), while that covered by 50% of assessments is: catch/landings (1983-2004), SSB (1985-2005), recruitment (1984-2003) (Figure 5).
