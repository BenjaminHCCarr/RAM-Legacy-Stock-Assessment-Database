The knowledge-base for commercially-exploited marine stocks

In total, 324 recent stock assessments for 288 marine fish and 36 invertebrate populations are included in the RAM Legacy database (Version 1.0, 2010; Table S1). Together these comprise time series of catch/landings for 308 stocks (95%), SSB estimates for 271 stocks (84%), and recruitment estimates for 269 stocks (83%) (Table S1).
