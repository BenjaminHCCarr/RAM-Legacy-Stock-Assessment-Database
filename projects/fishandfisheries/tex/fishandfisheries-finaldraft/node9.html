Assessing the status of commercially exploited marine stocks

We evaluate the status of assessed stocks overall and by management body, using standard reference points so that all stocks are referenced to a comparable benchmark. Following [142010Froese and Proelß] and [662009Worm et al.Worm, Hilborn, Baum et al.], we compare the current biomass and exploitation rate of stocks relative to their MSY reference points, and , respectively.

We do not advocate the use of MSY targets for management, but still report MSY-related BRPs because they are the most commonly estimated BRP and can be used to compare multiple stocks.
