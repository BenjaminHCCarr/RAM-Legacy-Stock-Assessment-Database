Taxonomic coverage

The taxonomic coverage of the database includes 157 species from 57 families (Figure B.2). This comprises a relatively small proportion of caught taxa and a smaller proportion of marine fish biodiversity (Figure B.2).

Four taxonomic orders (Gadiformes, Pleuronectiformes Perciformes and Scorpaeniformes) account for 67% of available stock assessments.
