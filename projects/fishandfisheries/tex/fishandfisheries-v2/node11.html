Knowledge-base analyses

The holdings of assessments are summarised by their geographic coverage, their management regime, the type of assessment used. We also identify assessments where other life-history parameters are identified in the reference assessment document. The available timeseries for different stocks are examined in terms of their duration in years and first and last years of assessment time window.

Taxonomic coverage in the database was compared to the diversity of marine fishes in global fisheries landings (as represented by the SAUP database) and all marine fishes (as represented by FishBase).
